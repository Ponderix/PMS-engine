This website is a political map simulator for a First-Past-The-Post style election map of Adoria. You can fill in the districts by selecting a colour with the "colour picker" and clicking a district. To fill in multiple district when hovering over them press "F", to un-toggle this mode press "C". To fill in the entire map with a clour, select the colour and press "D".
The counter which shows how many districts are filled in with a certain colour does not work yet.
